### VIPs Only

All Trainers traveling to [Indigo Plateau](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Indigo_Plateau "Indigo Plateau") must present their Badges to prove their worth. The first guard looks to see if a Trainer has earned the [Boulder Badge](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Badge#Boulder_Badge "Badge"). The other guards are posted at various checkpoints farther along the route to inspect each Trainer for the other seven Badges. Once the last guard has verified that you have earned the [Earth Badge](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Badge#Earth_Badge "Badge"), you are free to enter Victory Road.

### Victory Road

There are several large boulders in [Victory Road](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Victory_Road_(Kanto) "Victory Road (Kanto)") that can only be moved with [Strength](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Strength_(move) "Strength (move)"), like in the [Seafoam Islands](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Seafoam_Islands "Seafoam Islands"). However, instead of dropping them down through holes in the floor, they must be moved onto pressure switches to unlock new areas.

#### 1F

Near the entrance, there is a boulder to the west and a pressure switch to the east. To activate the switch, use [Strength](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Strength_(move) "Strength (move)") to move the boulder eastward (↓1, →5, ↑2, →7, ↑2, →1, and ↓1). Doing so removes the barrier on the rocky ridge. Head to the northeast area to find two items near the north wall. The cave's narrow layout forces you to use the boulder here to block off one item in order to reach the other. Collect either the [Rare Candy](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Rare_Candy "Rare Candy") or [TM02](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/TM02 "TM02") ( [Dragon Claw](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Dragon_Claw_(move) "Dragon Claw (move)")), then exit and re-enter the cave to reset the puzzle and reach the other item. Go west past the two Cooltrainers and climb up the ladder to the northwest.

#### 2F

Another boulder can be found to the south. Push this one southwestward (←2, ↓2, ←2) to reach the switch and clear the next barrier. Walk along the rocky ridge and go down the first stairway. Pick up [TM37](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/TM37 "TM37") ( [Sandstorm](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Sandstorm_(move) "Sandstorm (move)")) to the west, then head northeastward to reach a [Full Heal](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Full_Heal "Full Heal") and [TM07](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/TM07 "TM07") ( [Hail](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Hail_(move) "Hail (move)")). Climb the northeast ladder to the third floor.

#### 3F

Go northeast to fight a Cooltrainer♂ and pick up a [Max Revive](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Max_Revive "Max Revive"). Head to the northwest area to find [TM50](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/TM50 "TM50") ( [Overheat](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Overheat_(move) "Overheat (move)")) and another switch. Climb down the ladder to reach a separate part of the second floor.

#### 2F

Battle PokéManiac Dawson and pick up the [Guard Spec.](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Guard_Spec. "Guard Spec.") in the crater to the east. Take the ladder back up to the third floor.

#### 3F

Go back to the boulder in the northeast area and push it all the way westward (↑2, ←22, ↓1, ←4, ↓3, →1) to reach the switch. This removes the next barrier so that you can reach the southern area. Walk across the rocky ridge and go south to battle two more Cooltrainers. Head east and push the boulder here down the hole in the floor, then jump down after it.

#### 2F

Shove the boulder all the way westward to the switch. This removes the barrier near where the boulder landed. Climb up onto the ridge and take the ladder up to the third floor.

#### 3F (Southeast)

Climb down the next ladder back to the second floor.

#### 2F (East)

The Cooltrainer in the final area offers to teach [Double-Edge](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Double-Edge_(move) "Double-Edge (move)") to a compatible Pokémon, in recognition of you clearing the cave. Take the eastern exit to return to [Route 23](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Kanto_Route_23 "Kanto Route 23").

## Route 23 (North)

Follow the tiled pathway to the north and head through the maze of stones to finally reach [Indigo Plateau](https://bulbapedia.bulbagarden.net/wiki/Indigo_Plateau "Indigo Plateau").
